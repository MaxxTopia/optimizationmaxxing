Third-party sensor components bundled in resources/lhm
=======================================================

This app reads hardware sensors (CPU/GPU temperatures, voltages, fan speeds,
etc.) using the following third-party components, redistributed unmodified.

1) PawnIO (PawnIO_setup.exe)
   - Microsoft-signed kernel driver that runs sandboxed signed modules; the
     app communicates with it only over the device IO-control (IOCTL)
     interface.
   - License: GNU General Public License v2.0 or later, WITH a linking
     exception permitting independent programs that communicate through the
     IOCTL interface (and modules over the Pawn interface).
   - Source / corresponding source offer: https://github.com/namazso/PawnIO
     Installer: https://github.com/namazso/PawnIO.Setup

2) PawnIO modules (IntelMSR, AMD family modules, LpcIO, SMBus, RyzenSMU, ...)
   - Compiled Pawn bytecode embedded inside LibreHardwareMonitorLib.dll.
   - License: GNU Lesser General Public License v2.1.
   - Source: https://github.com/namazso/PawnIO.Modules

3) LibreHardwareMonitorLib.dll
   - License: Mozilla Public License 2.0 (file-level copyleft). Redistributed
     unmodified.
   - Source: https://github.com/LibreHardwareMonitor/LibreHardwareMonitor

No modifications were made to any of the above binaries. Their full license
texts are available at the source URLs listed.
